October Festival City Mall

Year of completion  :  2019
Project Type  :  Commercial and service mall.
Total Area  :  The project occupies an area of 11,000 square meters.
Number of Floors  :  The mall consists of five floors.

• Basement: This floor serves as a garage with the capacity to accommodate 100 cars and includes a supermarket store.
• Lower Ground Floor: This floor is described as "low-height," but its specific purpose or use is not mentioned. Typically, lower ground floors in malls may house facilities like storage, maintenance areas, or utility spaces.
• Upper Ground Floor: This floor is described as "high-height." It's common for this floor to include anchor stores, larger retail spaces, or entrances to the mall.
• First Floor: The first floor is dedicated to shops. This is where various retail outlets or stores are typically located, catering to shoppers.
• Last Floor: The last floor serves as apartments for management, which can be used as clinics or offices for companies. This indicates a mixed-use aspect of the mall, combining commercial and service functions.
As an architecture engineer for the owner, my role involves creating and revising shop drawings, ensuring quality standards are met, and approving subcontractor invoices. I oversee the construction process to align it with the owner's vision, budget, and quality expectations.
